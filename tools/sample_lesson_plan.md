# Lesson Plan

**Teacher:** Ms. Rivera
**Grade:** 5
**Subject:** Mathematics — Comparing Decimals (tenths and hundredths)
**Duration:** 45 minutes
**Format:** Whole-class number talk / guided discussion

## Learning objectives
- Students compare two decimals to the hundredths place and justify which is larger.
- Students explain reasoning using place value (not "more digits = bigger").

## Anticipated misconception
- Students think a number with more digits is larger (e.g., 0.45 > 0.5 because "45 > 5").

## Activity
Pose "Which is greater, 0.5 or 0.45?" Students turn-and-talk, then share. Teacher elicits
reasoning, revoices contributions, and presses students to justify with place value. Keep
participation equitable; use wait time after each question.

## Assessment / success
- Most students can articulate a place-value justification by the end.
- Every student contributes at least once.
